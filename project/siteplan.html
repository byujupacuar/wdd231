Site Name

saloniluminat.com was chosen for its elegance and direct association with luxury and personal care. It reflects the salon’s style and its mission to highlight beauty with a golden touch.

Site Purpose

The saloniluminat.com website provides information about beauty services such as haircuts, coloring, makeup, hair treatments, and online booking. It also includes a style gallery, client testimonials, and special promotions.

Scenarios

  • Can I book a hair treatment appointment for this weekend?
  • What makeup styles do you offer for special events?

Color Scheme

  • Black (#000000): Main background, headers, sections, and footer.
  • Gold (#d4af37): Text, borders, accents, and highlighted titles.

Typography

  • Playfair Display: For titles and headers, adds classic elegance.
  • Noto Sans: For body text, ensures modern readability.
